Frequently Asked Questions

What are the prerequisites for internship programs?

Basic knowledge of electronics/electrical concepts is preferred but not mandatory. We provide foundational training to ensure all students can participate effectively in our programs.

Do you provide certificates upon completion?

Yes, we provide industry-recognized certificates for all our training programs. These certificates are valuable additions to your academic and professional portfolio.

Can I customize DIY kits for my college curriculum?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating custom trainer kits that align with specific curriculum requirements. Contact us to discuss your needs and get a personalized solution.

What is the typical turnaround time for PCB manufacturing?

Standard orders typically take 5-7 business days. We also offer quick-turn services for urgent requirements with 24-48 hour delivery options.

Do you offer job placement assistance?

Yes, we provide comprehensive career support including resume building, interview preparation, and connections with our industry partner network for job opportunities.

Can I visit your facility before enrolling?

Certainly! We encourage prospective students to visit our facility. Contact us to schedule a tour and see our labs, equipment, and ongoing projects firsthand.
